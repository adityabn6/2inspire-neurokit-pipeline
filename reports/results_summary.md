## Headline: the coupling "finding" is a duration artifact

The first pass flagged breathing‚Äìcardiac coupling as *weaker* in self-selected
playing. Re-running on **duration-matched first-60 s windows** removes it:

| condition contrast (self vs reading) | full piece p_fdr | matched 60 s p_fdr |
|---|---|---|
| HR‚Äìresp HF coherence | **0.005** | 0.111 |
| HR‚Äìresp xcorr peak | **0.005** | 0.390 |

‚Üí the coupling difference tracked piece *length* (long free improvisations are
less stationary), not the condition. See `fig_coupling_duration.png` ‚Äî the
reading/self gap is large on full pieces and closes under matching.

## What survives duration matching (the real, if modest, signal)

| condition contrast (self vs reading) | effect (rank-biserial) | matched 60 s p_fdr |
|---|---|---|
| **mean HR** | ‚àí0.68 (lower in self-selected) | **0.022** |
| RSA P2T | +0.47 (higher in self-selected)¬π | 0.111 |
| RMSSD | +0.44 | 0.111 |
| respiration rate | ‚àí0.31 | 0.237 |

¬π RSA/RSP are whole-piece (from `neurokit_pipeline`), so not yet duration-matched.

Both surviving signals point the same way: **self-selected playing ‚Üí lower heart
rate and a (sub-threshold) higher-RSA / higher-RMSSD trend** = a calmer, more
parasympathetic state than sight-reading, consistent with hypothesis **H1**
(`reports/hypotheses_music_physiology.md`) and with reduced cognitive/visual load.
mean HR is the one metric to survive FDR in the matched family ‚Äî promising but to
be confirmed (it is one of several windows one could choose).

**Across all three pieces** (Friedman, full piece, p_fdr): mean HR 0.004,
respiration rate 0.0005, RSA P2T 0.048 differ; RMSSD does not. See
`fig_across_pieces.png`.

## Motor confound resolved: the lower HR is real, not movement

I√±esta (2008) warns that playing HR is partly physical effort. Using hand-motion
energy from the **Movella DOT sensors** (`motion_features.py`; acc-magnitude SD,
3 sensors/piece, matched 60 s):

- Self-selected playing has **more** hand motion than sight-reading (rank-biserial
  +0.52, p_perm 0.012) ‚Äî the confound's premise holds.
- Yet the **mean-HR effect strengthens after adjusting for motion**: condition
  coefficient ‚àí3.9 bpm (p=0.052) ‚Üí **‚àí5.8 bpm (p_fdr 0.042)** with motion in the
  model (LMM, participant random intercept).

So players **move more but beat slower** during self-selected playing ‚Äî the lower
HR is an autonomic effect, not reduced movement (if anything, motor effort masks
it). See `fig_motor_confound.png`. This is the strongest evidence for **H1**.

## Chroma ‚Üí physiology (H2): suggestive trend, not confirmed

Tested in piece 3 (the only place music varies between people) with the **correct
OLS** (one obs/participant ‚Üí a random effect is degenerate). Using the
**duration-corrected audio‚Üîphysiology mapping** (see next section) on music
windows **trimmed** to the actual playing:

- chroma entropy ‚Üí mean HR: **r=0.40, p=0.071** (Œ≤ +8.1; p=0.093 adjusted for
  tempo+loudness); chroma entropy ‚Üí RMSSD: **r=‚àí0.36, p=0.111**; note density ‚Üí
  HR (all pieces LMM) p=0.035. All point one way ‚Äî **more harmonic complexity ‚Üí
  more arousal** ‚Äî but **none survive FDR** (p_fdr ‚âà 0.21‚Äì0.35). See
  `fig_music_physio.png`.

Fixing the mapping + trimming nudged entropy‚ÜíHR from p‚âà0.15 to p‚âà0.07 and made the
directions consistent, **without manufacturing significance**. (Under the earlier
mis-specified mixed model this was a false "p_fdr=0.02".) **H2 stays exploratory** ‚Äî
a real lead for the manual-timestamp pass, not a result yet.

## Audio‚Üîphysiology mapping corrected (P18) + trimmed to music

The audio clips are numbered by video; we had assumed video k = Hexoskin piece k.
A duration check (`music_segments.py`) showed the audio music window matches the
Hexoskin duration within 15 s for 65/74 clips ‚Äî but caught two real problems
(`remap_audio.py`, conservative: only override on a >60 s identity mismatch with a
confident alternative):

- **P18** ‚Äî video order ‚âÝ piece order (video 1 = 709 s = piece 3); **remapped** so
  audio joins the correct physiology.
- **P1** ‚Äî all three pieces are one continuous clip; **excluded** from per-piece
  audio (needs manual timestamps).

All other participants keep the identity mapping. Audio features were re-extracted
on the trimmed `[music_start, music_stop]` windows. The manual timestamps (when
they arrive) drop into `audio_features.py --segments` and re-validate the same way.

## Caveats

- Exploratory; n ‚âà 22‚Äì24; small effects expected (Koelsch & J√§ncke 2015).
- The 60 s window is one defensible choice; sensitivity to window length is worth
  checking. RSA/RSP are not yet windowed.
- **Motor confound now addressed** (above): the mean-HR effect survives and
  strengthens after adjusting for hand-motion energy. Movement is a real covariate,
  not the explanation.
- No sub-second audio‚Üîphysiology sync ‚Üí all music‚Üîphysiology results are piece-level.

## Recommended next steps
